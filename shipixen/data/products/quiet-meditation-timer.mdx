## Product Overview

Need a meditation timer that keeps things quiet and focused? Mindfulness Bell - Quiet helps you meditate without distractions. No guided narrations, no clutter - just you and your mind. Start small (even a few minutes!) and build your practice at your own pace.

Why you’ll love this meditation timer:

- Peaceful Simplicity: Just set your session and breathe. Mindfulness Bell - Quiet stays silent until gentle bells remind you to begin/end.
- Personalize Your Space: Add ambient sounds (rain, waves) or interval bells to stay centered.
- Track Progress: Record sessions to Apple Health, see stats, and sync history via iCloud.
- 100% Private: No ads, no tracking. Your meditation time belongs to you.

Key Features:

- Customizable meditation timer with start/end bells
- Soothing ambience sounds (optional)
- Interval reminders during sessions
- Apple Health integration
- iCloud sync for session history
- Insightful statistics (streaks, total time)
- Zero distractions, zero data sharing

Mindfulness Bell - Quiet is built for meditators who value focus. I crafted this app for my own practice - and polished every detail so you can meditate exactly how you need.
