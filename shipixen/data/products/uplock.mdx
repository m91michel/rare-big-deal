## Product Details

Black Friday Offer — Uplock

Uplock is a fast, secure, privacy-first app for iPhone, iPad, Mac, Apple Watch, and Apple Vision Pro that keeps your IDs, credit cards, passwords, secure notes, and other sensitive documents safe. Everything lives on your device and syncs over iCloud with end-to-end encryption—with no accounts to create, no tracking, no third-party code, and no data collected. If you’re searching for a password manager, document vault, or secure storage app that is simple yet powerful, Uplock provides dependable protection ensured by Apple's industry-leading security features. Built in Montreal by a small independent team, Uplock delivers a lightweight, zero-knowledge, native experience and stands out as a smooth alternative to 1Password, Bitwarden, or LastPass.
