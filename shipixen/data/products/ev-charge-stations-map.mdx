## Product Overview

Key Features:

- Predicts mechanical energy use for vehicles that drive today, yesterday, and in the future.
- It receives route inputs and provides a kWh output.
- This is an app to show all electrical car charging stations through their location.
- Integration with Navigation Apps:  Navigate to selected charging stations using popular navigation apps like Apple Maps or Google Maps.
- Charging Station Compatibility: Help users identify stations compatible with their specific e-car model, connector type, and charging capabilities.
- Favorite Stations:  Users can mark charging stations as favorites, making it quicker for them to find stations they frequently use. 
- Route Planner: You can easily plan long-distance road trips with the A Better Route Planner infrastructured Route Planner engine.
- Vision Pro: You can plan your routes before your trips and save your planned route.

With EV Charge Stations Map, you can:
- Any route, any electric vehicle, any context.
- Find public charging stations compatible with your EV
- Check for station functionality and current availability
- Link to your favorite navigation app for directions to your selected charger
- Find all the charging stations along a road trip route
- And more!
