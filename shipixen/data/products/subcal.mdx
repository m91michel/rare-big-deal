Smart Dashboard
- View all subscriptions at a glance with beautiful status cards
- Track total monthly/yearly expenses with detailed analytics
- See which subscriptions are renewing soon or have expired
- One-tap editing and management for all your services

 Intelligent Notifications
- Get smart reminders before renewals (1, 3, or 7 days)
- Custom notification times that work with your schedule

Privacy First

- No account required - start tracking immediately
- Your subscription data stays private and secure

Perfect for:
- Busy professionals managing multiple subscriptions

- Families organizing household subscription services
- Anyone who wants to take control of their recurring payments
