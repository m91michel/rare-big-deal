## Product Overview

Turn your daily steps into cute rewards!
Steps & Beasts motivates you to walk more by turning your movement into a fun, collectible adventure.

WHY STEPS & BEASTS?  
Every morning starts with a new egg. As you walk and reach your step goal, it hatches into a unique Cute Beast. From playful pups to tiny dragons – collect them all! The more consistent you are, the rarer the Beasts you’ll discover. It’s the perfect blend of fitness and fun.

FEATURES YOU'LL LOVE:  

- COLLECT 200 UNIQUE BEASTS
Discover beautifully designed creatures, each full of personality and charm.

- DAILY STREAK REWARDS
Keep your streak alive to unlock rare and legendary Beasts.

- DETAILED STATISTICS
Track your daily steps, distance, calories, and 7-day averages — plus motivating charts and fun comparisons.

- WIDGETS THAT MOTIVATE
Watch your egg evolve as you move — right from your home screen.

- APPLE HEALTH & WEARABLE SUPPORT
Use your iPhone, Apple Watch or any Apple Health compatible wearable
