## Product Overview

Glu Sight – Smarter Diabetes Management Made Easy

Managing diabetes can be complex. Glu Sight helps simplify it. With a clean, intuitive design and powerful tracking features, Glu Sight helps you stay on top of your blood glucose and insulin therapy using clear data and personalized insights.

Whether you're managing type 1, type 2, pre-diabetes, or other forms of diabetes, Glu Sight gives you a better view of your health so you can make informed decisions, every day.


＊＊ FEATURES

Daily glucose insights  
- Track blood glucose and insulin trends using charts, averages, and estimated A1c  
- Identify recurring highs or lows with a visual heat map  
- View time-in-range summaries across custom periods

Fast and easy logging  
- Add glucose and insulin readings in seconds  
- Log context like meals, basal and bolus insulin, medications, and notes

Custom targets  
- Set your own glucose range for better insight  
- Adjust charts and highlights based on your personal goals

Helpful reminders  
- Set custom reminders for insulin, medications, or logging  
- Stay consistent with friendly notifications throughout the day

Apple Health integration  
- Sync data from supported CGMs, meters, and insulin pens  
- Compatible with Dexcom G5/G6/G7, Accu‑Chek Connect, InPen, OneTouch Verio Flex, and others  
- Automatically share your entries back to Apple Health if you choose

System features  
- Use Siri, Spotlight, and home screen widgets for quick actions  
- Customize app appearance, units (mg/dL or mmol/L), and logging experience

Private by default  
- Data stays on your device unless you enable iCloud sync  
- Built with privacy in mind and no third-party tracking
