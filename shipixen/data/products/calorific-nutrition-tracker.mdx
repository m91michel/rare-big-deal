## Product Overview

Say hello to Calorific, your new best friend in nutrition tracking! Whether you're counting calories, watching your macros, or just curious about what's on your plate, Calorific makes it super easy. Just open the app, snap a photo, and get instant nutritional insights. It’s like having a personal nutrition tracker in your pocket!

- Intelligent Meal Estimates: Point, shoot, and eat! Take a picture of your meal, fill in a few details, and Calorific will provide an educated guess on calories, protein, carbs, fats, and more. It’s like having an ai food tracker at your fingertips, helping you make informed choices effortlessly.

- Barcode Detection: Just snap a photo of any barcode, and Calorific will automatically detect it, fetch real nutrition information, and use that data to more accurately estimate your calories. Ideal for packaged foods, this feature ensures you always have the most precise information about what you’re eating.

- Search: Have you already eaten or just want to manually add an item? The new search feature makes it easy to quickly find what you’ve eaten.

- Apple Health integration: You can enable Health integration to automatically sync your nutritional information to the Apple Health app.

- Personal Health Plan: Create a scientifically optimized nutrition plan tailored to your body, goals, and lifestyle preferences.

- Calendar View: Keep track of your meals with our easy-to-use calendar. View your daily nutrition intake at a glance, see individual nutritional information for each meal, and edit entries as needed. Stay on top of your diet with a clear and organized daily overview.

- Camera Control: Easily log your meals right from your lock screen. Use the new Camera Control on the iPhone 16 (Pro) or add the lock screen widget for quick meal logging.

Tracking your nutrition has never been easier. No more manual entry, no more guesswork. Simply open the app, snap a pic, and let Calorific handle the rest.

Calorific uses advanced AI to estimate the nutrition of your meals and strives to provide accurate information by using real data whenever available, such as from barcodes. However, for precise dietary advice, please consult with a healthcare professional.
